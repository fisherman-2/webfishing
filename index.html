    Help/Info

    Hello and welcome to Web Fishing! This game is quite simple, but here are the basics: click "Cast" to cast your fishing pole, and you'll catch a random fish. You can then sell your fish for dabloons and get upgrades in the shop. Each fish has a rarity — Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Mythic, or Legendary — with different sell values. There's also a chance that the fish you catch has a mutation or variation. These are very rare and come in five tiers: Shiny, Golden, Shadow, Polychrome, and Radiant (the rarest), with a 1 in 180 chance of catching a Radiant fish. The rarest fish in the game is the **Radiant Blue Sea Dragon**, with odds of 0.000556% — that's 1 in 179,856! That should cover everything. If you enjoy this game/website, PLEASE visit my GitHub and star the repository. Okay, bye have fun!
